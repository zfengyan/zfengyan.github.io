About me

I am currently following the MSc Geomatics programme at TU Delft.

Geomatics

Studying in Geomatics has been an incredible learning experience thus far, albeit busy and challenging at times. As individuals, our understanding of the world around us varies, but integrating technology with our perceptual thinking allows us to gain a better perspective and affect how we live our lives. Geomatics is not merely a collection of techniques but a philosophy of life that is continually evolving.

The beginning of a journey is a moment to cherish forever. Looking back, I remember a time when I was a student lost in the vast ocean of knowledge and the complexities of life. The art of programming was a puzzle that seemed too daunting to solve, and my confidence waned with each attempt. But like a germinating seed, progress was made slowly, one step at a time, until it blossomed into a passion that I will always hold dear.
