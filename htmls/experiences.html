What it requires to successfully pursue a Ph.D. position

  • Strategic talk to a supervisor. What would you want to achieve during a conversation? After having a conversation, establish a shared document and shend it to the supervisor.
  • Respectful, gentle but not submissive.
  • Self-driven.
  • Exercising presenting skills - sometimes even more important than the content!
  • Cumulative writing skills.
  • Don't be afraid to say no when you need to as you are usually expected to work more and the professor usually don't know the specific workload.
  • Make friends.
  • Sometimes criticism is not personal but for improvement.
  • Diversify by developing multiple viable strategies to address a specific question.
